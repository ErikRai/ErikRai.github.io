Random Work

A Passion

No matter what form it came in, Art was always straightforward for me. All i need to know is, what's the end result supposed to resemble. In my early days when it came to doing GFX related work, i would wonder if i was skilled enough to design how my clients wants me to. After coming across a quote one day my perspective changed and i realized 2 things: I cant improve if i dont do the work & i shouldnt second guess myself.

The quote was - “The alternative to good design is always bad design. There is no such thing as no design.” - By Adam Something
